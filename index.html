On the Job Experience

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age: 18-29 years (Up to 35 for PWDs)
  • Education: Form 4 level or below
  • Status: Unemployed or underemployed
  • Location: All 47 counties (including refugees in Turkana & Garissa)

Key Program Points:

Duration: Socio-Emotional Skills Development (SESD) takes 20 days followed by On-the-Job Experience (apprenticeship) that lasts 5 months. The intervention combines on-the-job training with mentorship from industry experts.
Trade Selection: Participants can choose from various trades within digital, creative, green, blue, and grey economies.
Certification: Upon successful completion, participants receive formal recognition of their skills through Trade Testing assessment by NITA.
Support System: The program includes social-emotional skills development, covering areas such as communication, leadership, and financial literacy.

Implementation Status

NYOTA has successfully implemented Business Development Services (BDS) training across 46 counties, equipping thousands with foundational business skills and financial literacy.

KSh 267 Million Disbursed

In the Western Kenya cluster, a major milestone was reached with the disbursement of capital to 12,155 youth from Kakamega, Bungoma, Busia, and Vihiga counties.

Key Interventions

Business Development Services

Comprehensive training designed to equip youth with tools to start, manage, and grow successful enterprises.

Business Capital

Financial support including grants and seed capital tailored to the enterprise’s nature and growth potential.

Mentorship & Coaching

Structured mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs to help refine business models and navigate challenges.
